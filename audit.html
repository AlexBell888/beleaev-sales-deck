    WHAT WE MEASURE

    8 GEO signals.
    The ones that actually matter.

    Every AI engine weights these differently. Gaps in any one of them will cost you citations — and the pipeline behind them.

    Share of Model
    20/20
    The % of category-relevant AI answers that cite your brand. Category leaders sit at 30–45%. Everyone below 15% is effectively invisible.
    Schema Coverage
    15/15
    Organization, Product, Article, FAQPage, HowTo, Review, BreadcrumbList, WebSite. AI engines read structured data, not prose.
    Answer-Block Readiness
    15/15
    FAQPage schema, direct-answer patterns, Q&A sections, 40–60 word extractable passages. AI quotes blocks, not paragraphs.
    AI Crawler Access
    12/12
    Does your robots.txt allow GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, CCBot, Google-Extended? Blocking any one blocks you from that engine entirely.
    Entity Readiness
    12/12
    Canonical, meta description, OG tags, Twitter card, knowledge panel signals. The machine-readable identity AI systems verify against.
    Authority Signals
    10/10
    Mentions on G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, Wikipedia, tier-1 press. AI models train on sources they trust — and they trust these.
    Content Depth
    10/10
    Word count, original data, statistics, expert quotes. Thin content gets summarised; deep content gets quoted.
    Heading Hierarchy
    6/6
    One H1, multiple H2s, clean structure. A document AI can skim in under a second beats a flat wall of text every time.
