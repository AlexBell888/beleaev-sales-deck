WHO WE DO NOT WORK WITH

We say no more often than we say yes. Five types of engagement we will politely decline.

  • Brands that want a miracle in 30 days. GEO compounds. Meaningful citation lift takes 60–90 days minimum. If you need headline numbers by end of month, we are not your agency.
  • Brands that do not sign a data-access agreement. We need read access to your analytics, GA / Search Console / CRM, or we cannot prove attribution. No data, no engagement.
  • Brands whose product is genuinely bad. GEO amplifies what exists. If your NPS is negative and your reviews are warning signs, we will tell you to fix the product first. We are not here to polish a sinking ship.
  • Brands that want us to ghostwrite fake reviews or buy inauthentic citations. Hard no. Our team will not compromise the authority layer that makes GEO work in the first place.
  • Brands in sectors we will not take. Gambling (without sector specialism), adult content, crypto speculation, political advocacy. We keep our focus narrow on purpose.
