PRICING FAQ

Questions procurement will ask.

Why are prices shown as ranges, not fixed?
Each engagement differs by category complexity, competitor intensity, content volume, and regulatory depth. The range brackets 90% of engagements in that tier. We price inside the range on the proposal, based on the scope we agreed on the discovery call. No surprises later.
Can we cancel mid-term?
Minimum terms exist because meaningful GEO outcomes take 90 days minimum to measure. You can exit after the minimum term with 30 days' written notice. You keep the dashboard access until the notice period ends. Contractually, you own every asset we ship during the engagement.
What if we already have an SEO agency?
We work alongside SEO agencies all the time — technical SEO and GEO overlap on schema, page architecture, and core content work. We take the AI layer; your SEO agency keeps the blue-link layer. We'll happily document the handover so nothing duplicates.
Is there a setup fee?
No. The first month's fee covers the baseline audit, discovery workshops, dashboard provisioning, and content kickoff. If you prefer a one-off "sprint" engagement (audit + 3-month content programme, no minimum term), we can structure that too — ask on the discovery call.
Do you offer a trial?
Yes — the AI Visibility Snapshot is free and takes 5 working days. A 4-page report showing where you stand across the six AI engines, against your three nearest competitors. If you decide not to proceed, the report is yours to keep.
What about enterprise procurement requirements?
For Gold engagements we support vendor onboarding, DPIAs, GDPR/EU AI Act documentation, SOC 2 evidence, and the standard enterprise vendor questionnaire. Our legal team has sat through the Fortune 500 onboarding loop — we know the ropes.
